Get Ready!
  • Target Muscles: Forehead

  • 1. Sit upright and facing forward, with shoulders back.

  • 2. Place your hand on your forehead, with your top fingers against your hairline.

  • 3. Using both hands, pull your forehead and eyebrows upwards.

  • 4. Hold pose for 5 seconds.

  • 5. Release.
