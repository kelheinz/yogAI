Privacy Policy

At Yog.AI, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and disclose information when you use our Facial Yoga App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information: We may collect personal information that you provide to us voluntarily, such as your facial image and any other information you choose to provide when using the App but data is not intentionally stored for purposes other than the application's intended use.

1.2 Facial Data: In order to provide the facial yoga features, the App may collect and process facial data, such as frame images or videos of your face. This data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. Use of Information

2.1 Facial Data: Facial data collected by the App is used solely for the purpose of providing facial yoga exercises and evaluating your progress. This data is processed locally on your device and is not not retained by our servers. No facial data will be stored or retained so that no biometric coordinates can be extracted from remaining facial images.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Service Providers: We will not share personal information and usage information with any third-party service providers.

3.2 MediaPipe API: As of November 2021, MediaPipe APIs list in their terms of services that when using the API's, processing of the input data occurs locally and never sent to Google servers. This guarantees no leakage of data to third-party services but should this change, notifications will be posted to this Privacy Policy and its terms.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under the age of 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
